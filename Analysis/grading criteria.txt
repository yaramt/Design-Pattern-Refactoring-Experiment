Grade 0:

The participant does not attempt to address the prompt.
There is no evidence of understanding or effort to follow the instructions.
The response is entirely incorrect or incomplete.

Grade 1:

The participant partially addresses the prompt but misses key elements or makes significant errors.
There is some evidence of understanding, but it is incomplete or inaccurate.
The response may contain some correct information but lacks clarity or coherence.

Grade 2:

The participant fully addresses the prompt with accurate and complete information.
There is clear evidence of understanding, and the response aligns well with the instructions.
The response is comprehensive, coherent, and demonstrates proficiency in following the instructions.
